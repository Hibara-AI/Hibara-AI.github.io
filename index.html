About



Hi there! I am a master student at Department of Computer Science, Rutgers University. Simultaneously, I'm a research intern on NLP at Recurrent.ai, advised by Zhilin Yang.

My research interest revolves around Machine Learning, Cognitive Science and NLP.
But ultimately, my dream is to explore the computational philosophy of interaction between real world and the grand, intricate but captivating human mental world and theological terms beyond it, if it could ever exist, together with human senses(visions and linguistics are being widely studied in computer science regime, but there still remains imagine, dream, psychology, etc). As I call it, "decode the God, or at least his shadow"
One's full awareness rings that this might never be achieved by human civilization known so far, but one would always welcome anyone to have a conversation on it with some whiskey. Cheers!

Prior to coming to Rutgers, I was an undergraduate student at Tongji University, major in Electrical Information Engineering and Applied Mathematics.
For personal leisure, I enjoy practicing the piano(classical), reading about philosophy, history and Chinese ancient literature, doing sports and hiking.
You can contact me through A@B, where A=charles.deng and B=rutgers.edu.
